Dash0 is being trialed as our unified observability platform, replacing a fragmented setup of Prometheus, Grafana, and ELK. Its OpenTelemetry-native approach, combined with built-in APM, log management, and infrastructure monitoring, has already helped the 911 team reduce mean time to detection for performance regressions.
