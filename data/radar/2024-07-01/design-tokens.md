Design tokens have moved to trial after successful prototyping. We now generate platform-specific tokens (CSS custom properties, Swift constants, Kotlin values) from a single source using Style Dictionary. The 911 team has integrated token consumption into their build pipeline, ensuring design consistency across web and native platforms.
