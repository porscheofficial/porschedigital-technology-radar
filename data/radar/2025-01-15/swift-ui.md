SwiftUI is being assessed for our iOS application modernization. The Cayenne team is prototyping the vehicle remote control interface using SwiftUI, evaluating its declarative approach against our existing UIKit codebase. Early results show faster UI iteration cycles but some limitations with complex custom animations.
