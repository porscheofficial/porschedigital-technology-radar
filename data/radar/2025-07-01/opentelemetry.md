OpenTelemetry is being assessed as a vendor-neutral observability framework. While we use Datadog for monitoring, adopting OTel instrumentation would reduce vendor lock-in and provide portable telemetry data. The 911 and Macan teams are prototyping OTel SDK integration in two services to evaluate the migration path from Datadog-native instrumentation.
