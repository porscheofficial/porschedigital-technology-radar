TypeScript continues in trial with expanding adoption. The Cayenne team has started migrating their Vue.js codebase to TypeScript. Our shared type definitions between frontend and backend (via tRPC) have eliminated an entire class of integration bugs. We expect to move TypeScript to adopt in the next radar cycle.
